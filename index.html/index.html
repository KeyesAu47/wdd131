Plain City, Utah

Plain City, Utah, is a small rural community in Weber County, located about 10 miles northwest of Ogden. Founded in 1859, it retains its agricultural roots, offering scenic views of the Wasatch Mountains and open plains. With a population of around 7,000, the city boasts a close-knit, family-oriented atmosphere. Known for its quiet, relaxed lifestyle, Plain City features parks, local events like Pioneer Day celebrations, and easy access to outdoor recreation, making it a charming and peaceful place to live or visit.
